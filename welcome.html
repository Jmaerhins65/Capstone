Welcome to NEUScholar: A WEB PLATFORM FOR THESIS ACCESS AND RETRIEVAL SYSTEM FOR THE NEU MAIN LIBRARY, the leading resource for locating, disseminating, and conserving scholarly works. Our platform is committed to giving educators, researchers, and students a smooth and simple experience.

Explore a wide range of theses and dissertations from a variety of academic disciplines to learn about a wealth of knowledge. Our broad archive has everything you need, whether you're looking for ideas for your own research or want to explore the most recent scholarly findings.


    Become a Part of Our Academic Community: Make connections with students and researchers, share ideas, and work together on projects. We can push the boundaries of knowledge together.

    We appreciate your participation in NEUScholar. We're excited to help you with your academic pursuits and build a strong academic community.

    Contribute to the academic community by sharing your own research by uploading theses and dissertations. Sharing your work ensures that important knowledge is preserved for future generations and aids others in their research endeavors.

    Simple Navigation and Access: Finding and accessing the information you require is made easy by our user-friendly interface. Finding relevant studies has never been simpler thanks to the well-organized categories and refined search options.

    Resources and Support: Do you require help? Our support staff is available to assist you! We offer a variety of resources to make sure you get the most out of our platform, from research advice to submission guidelines.
